# Is Book Compatible with Digital Garden?

In a book, the thought processing is linear, you often need to read front to
back to grasp the entire concept. Later chapters in books build up on top of
earlier chapters. This reading paradigm is opposite to [[digital-garden]], where
you are invited to wander around.
