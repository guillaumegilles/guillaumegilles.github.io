Exercice 1

According to a study conducted by an insurance company: the distance in km before having the first accident follows a normal distribution of average 35000 and standard deviation 5000.

What is the percentage of drivers who have the first accident before 25000 km? What is the percentage of drivers who have the first accident between 25000 and 40000 km? Thanks to this change of variable, we obtain that рқ‘Ң follows a reduced centered normal law.   рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ӣ вүӨ 25000) = 1 вҲ’ рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ң вүӨ 2) = 0.02275 рқ‘ғ (25000 вүӨ рқ‘Ӣ вүӨ 40000) = рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ң вүӨ 1) вҲ’ (1 вҲ’ рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ң вүӨ 2)) = 0.81859 рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ӣ вүҘ 45000) = 1 вҲ’ рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ң вүӨ 2) = 0.02275 We seek to calculate рқ‘Ҙ such that рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ӣ вүӨ рқ‘Ҙ) = рқ‘ғ (рқ‘Ң вүӨ (рқ‘Ҙ вҲ’ 35000)/5000) = 0.75 According to the table of the reduced centered normal law, it can be deduced that  :
